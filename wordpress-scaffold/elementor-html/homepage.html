About RFCI

The voice of
resilient flooring.

Est. 1976 · LaGrange, Georgia

Founded in 1976, RFCI is the non-profit trade association representing and protecting the resilient flooring industry in ways that no single manufacturer can.

From setting technical standards to running third-party certification programs, we advance the category for the entire built environment.

Learn more about RFCI

Our Strategic Focus

  1. 01 Advocacy & Engagement

    Representing the resilient flooring industry on legislative, regulatory, and public policy issues at the federal and state level.

  2. 02 Technical Standards

    Developing and maintaining the performance standards that define product quality, safety, and installation best practices.

  3. 03 Sustainability Programs

    Running FloorScore, ASSURE, AFFIRM, and EPDs, the programs that validate environmental and health performance for specifiers worldwide.

  4. 04 Promotion & Education

    Advancing industry knowledge through continuing education, original research, and market promotion to the architecture and design community.

  5. 05 Member Development

    Growing and supporting a membership of leading manufacturers and supply chain partners who shape the future of resilient flooring.

Sustainability

Sustainability programs you can rely on

RFCI manages the flooring industry's most trusted sustainability programs, built on rigorous testing, transparency, and continuous improvement.

FloorScore®

CA 01350 Emissions Standard
SCS Global Certification Body

FloorScore is the flooring industry's most recognized indoor air quality certification, verifying that products meet stringent VOC emissions standards for healthier interiors.

  • Recognized by LEED, WELL, BREEAM, CHPS, Green Globes, and other green building systems.
  • Includes independent review, periodic manufacturing audits, and annual compliance renewal.
Explore FloorScore

ASSURE® Certified

WPC + SPC Rigid Core Products
100PPM Max Heavy Metals

ASSURE was developed by RFCI to establish uniform quality standards for rigid core luxury flooring sold in North America, regardless of where it is manufactured.

  • Combines IAQ, performance, chemistry, and supply-chain testing with third-party certification.
  • Supports specifier and consumer confidence in the fastest-growing resilient flooring segment.
Explore ASSURE

AFFIRM™ Certified

ANSI Accredited Standard
Level 1 + 2 Certification Tiers

AFFIRM is a multi-attribute sustainability certification for resilient flooring products, covering environmental, health and wellness, and social impact categories.

  • Built on an ANSI-accredited consensus standard with independent third-party verification.
  • Designed to give designers, facility teams, and procurement professionals clearer sustainability validation.
Explore AFFIRM

Environmental Product Declarations

ISO 14025 EPD Standard
9 Product Types Covered

RFCI publishes industry-wide Environmental Product Declarations for nine resilient flooring product types, helping teams compare environmental impact data with more confidence.

  • Supports LEED, Green Globes, and other materials transparency initiatives.
  • Updated 2024 EPDs reflect current manufacturing data across resilient flooring categories.
Explore EPDs

Ecomedes is RFCI's product transparency and sustainability data platform. It gives architects, designers, and specifiers instant access to environmental certifications, EPDs, HPDs, and green building credit data for resilient flooring products.
